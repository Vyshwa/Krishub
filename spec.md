# KrishTech Computers Business Website

## Overview
A professional business website for KrishTech Computers, an IT company specializing in hardware and software solutions. The website features a clean, modern design with blue and white corporate styling.

## Design Requirements
- **Color Theme**: Blue and white corporate color scheme
- **Layout**: Card-based sections and well-structured layouts
- **Typography**: Professional fonts with clear headings
- **Style**: Clean, minimal text, responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Aesthetic**: Corporate IT look with modern visual elements

## Pages and Content

### Home Page
- Company introduction and overview
- Service highlights with call-to-action buttons
- Modern banner section
- Key visual elements showcasing the company

### About Us Page
- Company overview and background
- Mission statement focusing on technology and client trust
- Professional presentation of company values

### Software Solutions Page
Two main software offerings displayed in card format:

**Reveal**
- RFID-based software solution
- Scans sealed boxes for inventory management
- Real-time data visibility and tracking
- Targeted at import/export businesses

**Renote**
- Business management software
- Features for poster design, estimation, and billing
- Designed for individuals and small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs)

### Hardware Services Page
Comprehensive hardware offerings including:
- Computers and Laptops
- Speakers, Mouse, Keyboard
- SSD, RAM, SMPS (power supplies)
- General hardware sales, service, and technical support
